Sign in to your secondary Amazon account in the embedded browser. Complete any 2FA / new-device verification. When the homepage loads, click Save session.

What this does

The server runs a real Chromium under a virtual display (Xvfb) and streams it here via noVNC. When you save, the server exports the session cookies to data/storage_state.json and the daily scraper switches to that logged-in session — bypassing the IP-throttling we hit while scraping anonymously.

Don't log into your primary Amazon account from this tab. Use a separate, throwaway account with a different email / phone / payment method. See README for the safety rationale.
